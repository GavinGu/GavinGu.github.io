Proejct:

           

TagAssist

Our TagAssist project is an ongoing work on building a robust baggage tracking and localization system in the airport. The mission of this project is two fold. On one hand, TagAssist realizes departure-to-destination baggage status surveillance, which help to precisely knowing the baggage status in a real-time manner. On the other hand, TagAssist leverages Passive Radio Frequency Identification(RFID) technique for localzing the baggage on the baggage sorting pool, which shortens the sortting time, and enhances the baggage sorting efficiency. Up till now, we have prototyped our TagAssist system in Terminal One, Beijing Capital International Airport and Sanya Phenix Airport.

Collaborator: Tsinghua University, and sponsored by Hainan Airline, which is the third largest airline companies in China.
