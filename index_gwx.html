Projects and Internship

  • CitySee: Sep., 2012 - June, 2013, Hong Kong and Wu Xi

    • An urban carbon dioxide monitoring system (1000+ sensor nodes), in which I designed and implemented a time-efficient protocol for collision resolution [C2], designed a control plane based on preamble of data packets [C1] and analyzed the data collected from the sensor nodes to measure the system.

  • GreenOrbs: Sep., 2011 - Sep., 2012, Hong Kong and Wu Xi

    • A long-term wireless sensor network system in the vast forest (330 nodes). I designed and implemented a protocol for improving channel utilization with dedicated observation in PHY layer for wireless networks [C3] and conduct data analysis and network measurement based on the system.

  • Intel: Jun., 2010 - Nov., 2010, Shanghai

    • I worked as a member of the SCG group and helped build the cloud platform for software testing.
